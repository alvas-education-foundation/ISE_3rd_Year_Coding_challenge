Steps to prepare the dish

  1. Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds and let it splutter.
  2. Now add curry leaves, ginger, green chilli, chana dal and urad dal,
    Saute them well.
  3. When the seeds begin to splutter,add coriander leaf, add peanuts, turmeric powder followed by rice. Turn around a few times,
    so the rice is completely mixed.
  4. Add salt and lemon juice to the rice and mix well.
